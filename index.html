Comfort Experience

Anxiety-Free Care, By Design

Your comfort isn't an afterthought — it's the foundation of everything we do. From ceiling-mounted entertainment to in-office IV sedation, we've designed every detail around your peace of mind.

01

Nitrous Oxide

Safe, effective "laughing gas" that helps you relax during treatment. Effects wear off as soon as you leave the treatment room.

02

In-Office IV Sedation

Our licensed CRNA safely performs IV sedation while our dentists focus entirely on your care. Ideal for anxiety or complex procedures.

03

Hospital Dentistry

For patients with complex medical needs, care is provided locally at Gunnison Valley Hospital under general anesthesia — no need to travel north.

04

In-Office Entertainment

Ceiling-mounted televisions with local cable keep you comfortable and distracted during your visit. Your children will love our activity room.

Technology With Purpose

Better Tools, Better Outcomes

We invest in technology not for its own sake, but because it means less discomfort, faster treatment, and more accurate care for you.

Less Waiting

CEREC Same-Day Crowns

Digital scanning, in-office milling, and same-appointment placement. No messy impressions, no two-week wait.

Less Radiation

Digital X-Rays & 3D CBCT

Up to 90% less radiation than traditional X-rays. Instant chairside images and comprehensive 3D cone beam imaging.

Less Discomfort

Laser Dentistry

Diode and CO2 soft tissue lasers for precise, minimally-invasive procedures. Some patients don't even need anesthesia.

Better Understanding

Chairside Monitors

See your X-rays and treatment plans on screen alongside your doctor. Informed decisions, every time.

More Accurate Care

3D Cone Beam Imaging

Complete information from every angle for confident evaluations and treatment planning — with less radiation than a CAT scan.

Better Outcomes

Proven CEREC Results

More than 30 years of development with a proven long-term survival rate of 95% — restorations you can trust.
